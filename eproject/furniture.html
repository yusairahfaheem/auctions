ABOUT FURNITURE AUCTION

Buying furniture at auction can be the first step to transforming your living space. Some auction houses specialize in vintage and antique furniture sales or offer dedicated events. Many sellers provide previews to collectors before a furniture auction. This gives potential bidders the chance to inspect lots and ask questions. Alternately, collectors can explore online furniture auctions and browse by seller, style, period, and price point.
