VINTAGE ACCESSORIES

Vintage Accessories includes items from the 1920's onwards, Particularly sought after from this period are beaded dresses and opera coats, once donned by young women known as flapper girls. Dresses were worn loose to enable dancing, particularly the Charleston. Coco Chanel opened her Paris shop in the first year of the decade, her invention of 'the little black dress' is just part of the legacy that means she is still an icon today In contrast to the previous decade, the bias cut dresses of the 1930's were slim line, daringly low cut or backless. Naked shoulders were covered by a bolero or shrug, Elsa Shaparelli, an Italian whose ideas fitted the zeitgeist, rivalled Chanel, designing for Hollywood, Norman Hartnell was Queen Elizabeth's choice
