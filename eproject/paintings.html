Paintings

We hold monthly sales of decorative pictures as part of our Pre-loved and Vintage auctions and sales of selected fine art as part of our quarterly fine sales. These sales include oil paintings, watercolours and prints. While fashions have changed over time, good quality oil paintings and art works by established artists are always popular with our database of buyers from all over the world. Each sale comprises a wide selection of periods, styles and media by many different artists from different periods. Four times a year we also hold dedicated sales of contemporary art. Additionally there are often single artists collection auctions held at our saleroom.
