Chinese Stone Guanyin Head Sculpture

DESCRIPTION:

The Chinese Stone Guanyin Head Sculpture is a beautifully crafted artwork that depicts the head of the revered Buddhist deity, Guanyin. This sculpture is made of high-quality stone, which has been carefully carved and polished to bring out the intricate details and textures of the deity's face. Guanyin, also known as Avalokiteshvara, is a central figure in Mahayana Buddhism and is often depicted as a female bodhisattva. She is revered for her compassion and mercy, and is believed to have the power to hear and answer prayers.
