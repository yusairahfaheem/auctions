The Jews Wailing Place, Jerusalem', water-colour, signed

DESCRIPTION

William Simpson, British 1823-1899, 'The Jews Wailing Place, Jerusalem', water-colour, signed, dated and titled 1896, 9 x 15", William Simpson's watercolor masterpiece, "The Jews Wailing Place, Jerusalem" from 1896, reflects his British artistry. Measuring 9x15 inches, the piece is a poignant portrayal of Jerusalem's iconic site, skillfully capturing historical and cultural nuances.
