DESCRIPTION

Frederick William Jackson (British, 1859-1918), landscape with geese to foreground, oil on board, signed lower left, 24 x 32cm, framed and glazed. Frederick William Jackson's "Landscape with Geese to Foreground," an oil on board masterpiece from 1859-1918, showcases his British artistic prowess. Signed and framed, the 24x32cm painting captures serene beauty with meticulous detail and evocative scenes of nature.
