STILL LIFE WITH TEAPOT AND FRUIT (VERSO)

DESCRIPTION

SAMUEL JOHN PEPLOE, R.S.A. | STILL LIFE WITH ROSES (RECTO); STILL LIFE WITH TEAPOT AND FRUIT (VERSO) Samuel John Peploe's artistic finesse shines in "Still Life with Roses" on the recto side and "Still Life with Teapot and Fruit" on the verso. This double-sided masterpiece reveals Peploe's versatility, capturing the essence of still life with vibrant roses, a teapot, and luscious fruits. His skillful brushwork and attention to detail demonstrate a harmonious blend of color and composition, showcasing the artist's enduring impact within the realm of still life painting. The work exemplifies Peploe's ability to infuse everyday objects with beauty and significance, creating a captivating visual narrative that transcends the ordinary.
